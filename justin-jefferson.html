Contract Drama in Minnesota

Justin Jefferson, one of the NFL’s top receivers, is in the midst of contract negotiations with the Minnesota Vikings. Rumors suggest he’s seeking a deal that could reset the wide receiver market. His consistency, route-running, and explosive plays make him a top fantasy option—especially in PPR leagues.
